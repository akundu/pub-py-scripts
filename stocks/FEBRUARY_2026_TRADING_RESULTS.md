## Risk Considerations

### Known Risks

1. **Market Crash Risk** (-10% NDX move in 1 day)
   - Probability: ~0.1% per year
   - Impact: All 50 positions breached = -$100,000 (100% capital)
   - Mitigation: Stop losses, position size limits

2. **Volatility Spike**
   - Higher IV = wider percentile boundaries
   - May not find spreads meeting criteria
   - Missed opportunities on high-vol days

3. **Gap Risk** (Overnight/Weekend)
   - 1 DTE positions hold overnight
   - Weekend gaps (Friâ†’Mon positions)
   - Mitigated by p99 buffer (very wide strikes)

4. **Liquidity Risk**
   - Wide bid/ask spreads on NDX options
   - Slippage on entry/exit
   - Estimated 5-10% impact on credits

5. **Black Swan Events**
   - 2008 crisis: -20% day
   - 2020 COVID: -12% day
   - These exceed p99 by far
   - Would result in full capital loss

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Position Sizing**
   - Never risk >10% on single day
   - Use 5 contracts per spread max (vs 50 total)
   - Keep 50% cash reserve

2. **Stop Losses**
   - Exit if underlying within 50% of short strike
   - Don't wait for full breach
   - Limits max loss to 50% instead of 100%

3. **Diversification**
   - Mix 0DTE, 1DTE, 3DTE strategies
   - Don't put all capital in one DTE
   - Spread across multiple underlyings (SPX, RUT)

4. **Dynamic Position Sizing**
   - Reduce size after losing days
   - Increase after winning streaks
   - Kelly Criterion: ~10-15% of capital
