  Critical Finding: The "Win Rate Trap"

  An 85-90% win rate is NOT enough for profitability with credit spreads!

  The data shows:
  - At 100% win rate → Always profitable
  - At 85-91% win rate → Often losing money (losses are much larger than wins)
  - The profit factor must be > 1.0 to be profitable

  Why Intraday Extreme > Close-to-Close:

  The Intraday Extreme metric is more relevant for 0DTE options because:
  1. It captures the maximum intraday movement (low to high)
  2. Options expire at market close, so the max intraday move is the real risk
  3. Close-to-close can miss big intraday swings that recovered

  Key Observations

  Hourly - DOWN Days:
  - Morning shows strong negative momentum (mean -0.66% from 9:30 AM)
  - Reversal tendency: 42-50% of intraday moves are UP despite the day closing down
  - By afternoon, intraday movement becomes near-neutral (+0.00% mean at 3:30 PM)

  Hourly - UP Days:
  - Morning shows positive momentum (mean +0.37% from 9:30 AM)
  - Fade into close: Up % drops from 73.5% at open to only 40.6% at 3:30 PM
  - Last 90 minutes show slight negative drift (-0.03% to -0.07% mean)

  Last 30 Minutes - DOWN Days:
  - Bounce pattern: Mean turns slightly positive (+0.01% to +0.04%)
  - 3:50 PM shows 67.9% positive bars - strongest reversal signal
  - Suggests short covering / dip buying in final minutes

  Last 30 Minutes - UP Days:
  - Fade pattern: Consistent selling pressure (-0.05% to -0.07% mean)
  - Only 21.9% to 40.6% of bars are positive
  - Profit-taking evident as day closes

  Trading Insight:
  - On down days, the last 10-15 minutes often see a bounce
  - On up days, the last 30 minutes often see selling/profit-taking
  - Both patterns suggest mean reversion in the final trading window
